About me

I'm Alex, FrontEnd Development. My passion lies in creating user-centred experiences as a UI/UX designer and frontend developer. With a keen attention to detail and a love for clean code, I specialise in building high-performance websites and applications. My experience spans HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Gulp, Sass, jQuery, and Bootstrap. From concept to implementation, I strive to create digital experiences that seamlessly blend functionality and aesthetics. My journey is not limited to just coding. Collaboration is at the heart of my approach, as I believe the best results are achieved through teamwork and communication. Outside the realm of technology, I find solace in creative pursuits. Graphic design brings out my creativity in the digital realm. And when I need to recharge, you'll often find me playing video games. So whether you're looking for an experienced developer, creative designer or team player, I'm ready to bring your digital ideas to life. Let's embark on an exciting innovation journey together!
